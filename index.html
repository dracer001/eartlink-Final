FAQs

To register for a course at Eartlink Training Institute, follow these steps:
  1. Visit our website and navigate to the "Courses" section.
  2. Select the course you are interested in.
  3. Click on the "Register" button.
  4. Fill out the registration form with your details.
  5. Complete the payment process to confirm your enrollment.

The prerequisites for enrolling in a course at Eartlink Training Institute vary depending on the course. Generally:
  • For beginner courses, no prior experience is required.
  • Intermediate and advanced courses may require basic knowledge or experience in the relevant field.
  • Specific prerequisites are listed on the course description page. Make sure to review them before registering.

Eartlink Training Institute offers both online and in-person courses to accommodate different learning preferences. You can choose the format that best suits your schedule and learning style. The mode of delivery for each course is specified in the course description.

We provide comprehensive support for our students, including:
  • Access to course materials and resources.
  • Regular Q&A sessions with instructors.
  • Discussion forums to interact with peers.
  • Technical support for any issues related to course access or materials.
  • Career counseling and job placement assistance upon course completion.

Yes, Eartlink Training Institute offers a satisfaction guarantee. If you are not satisfied with a course, you can request a refund within the first 14 days of enrollment. Please refer to our refund policy on the website for more details on the process and conditions.
